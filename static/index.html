🌿 Mindful Eating Tips

  • 🍄 Put your fork down between each bite to slow your pace.
  • 💦 Chew each mouthful thoroughly - aim for 20 to 30 chews.
  • 👀 Notice the colours, textures, and aromas of your food.
  • 🔋 Eat away from screens to give your meal your full attention.
  • 🫲 Check in with your hunger level every few minutes.

A gentle reminder: it takes about 20 minutes for your brain to register fullness.
